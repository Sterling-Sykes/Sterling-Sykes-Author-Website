About the Author

Sterling Sykes is a fiction author driven by an insatiable love for character-driven fantasy and science fiction. His stories often dwell in the space between sacrifice and wonder, exploring the internal battle between light and shadow. After years of private writing, Sterling is now emerging from the creative cave to share his world with readers who crave storytelling that dares to feel deeply.
